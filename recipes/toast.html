Toast of Toasts

When you don't really want just bread anymore, try this recipe to turn bread into something completely different!

Ingredients

  • Some money
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tbs of butter

Directions

  1. Go to the store and buy a device called a toaster. You can find them for relatively cheap and don't need an expensive kind for this recipe!
  2. Unbox and plug in your toaster.
  3. Put the 2 slices of bread into the toaster and use it (Read the directions for your toaster as needed)
  4. Once the toaster is done, add 1 tbs of butter to each piece of toast and smear it around to cover the toast from end to end.
  5. Enjoy!
