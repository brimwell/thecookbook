Beef Recipe

When your neighbor gives you a hunk of raw beef, do something besides throw it out! Now you can follow this recipe to turn it into something useful!

Ingredients

  • 1-5 lbs of beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Pat down your beef until dry.
  3. Cover the beef liberally with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook beef for 15 minutes at 450, then turn down to 250 degrees.
  5. Check temperature of beef every 20 min or so until it gets to 120 degrees.
  6. Let rest for 20 minutes.
  7. Slice.
  8. Enjoy!
