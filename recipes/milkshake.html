Milkshake of Milkshakes

Turn boring kitchen ingredients into something extremely yummy with this fantastic new recipe!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Chocolate, vanilla, or strawberry ice cream

Directions

  1. Place ice cream into a container.
  2. Place milk into same container.
  3. Use a stick blender to combine the two.
  4. If too runny, use more ice cream, if too thick use more milk.
  5. Enjoy!
